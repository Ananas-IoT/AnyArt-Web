About us:

We are the team of enthusiasts from Lviv Polytechnic who are exited to help our city become a better place!

We are happy to welcome you at our website! There you can report about non-estethic places in Lviv, offer your help with solving it and vote for better solutions. Our dream is to make Lviv better for all of us: citizens and tourists both!
